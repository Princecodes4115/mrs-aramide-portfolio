    Naija Startups

    Naija startups started as a digital resource for small businesses, Naija Startups was formally launched in 2016, evolving into an Entrepreneur Support Organisation and digital hub with a mission to provide support for small to medium sized businesses and sustainability through its resources, network and valuable opportunities, thus moving the macroeconomic needle across geographies, on the African continent.

    Visit Naija Startups at naijastartups.co.

    Make Your Network Work For You

    Building a strong network and effectively leveraging it for the benefit of your business are important skills for every entrepreneur. From learning to play the long game to always improving your pitch to focusing more on what you can give rather than receive, networking is an art you must master if your goal is to be successful in business. In this episode of Top 5-in-5, Aramide Abe, Founder, Naija Startups, shares her top five tips on "Making Your Network Work For Your Business"
